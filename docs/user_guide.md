# User Guide - Credly

## What is Credly?

Credly is a simple ledger and order management app for a distributor to manage local shops. Track orders, payments, and outstanding balances all in one place.

---

## Getting Started

### Adding a Shop

1. From the main screen, tap the **+ Add Shop** button
2. Enter the shop name (required)
3. Enter the shop phone number (optional)
4. Tap **Save**

### Viewing Shops

The main screen shows all your shops with their current balance:
- Green balance = Shop owes little or nothing
- Red balance = Shop has outstanding dues

### Searching Shops

Use the search bar at the top to quickly find a shop by name.

---

## Orders

### Adding an Order

1. Tap on a shop to open its details
2. Tap **Add Order**
3. Select products and quantities
4. The total is calculated automatically
5. Tap **Save Order**

The order amount is automatically added to the shop's balance.

### Viewing Order History

All orders for a shop are listed in the shop's detail page, sorted by date (newest first).

---

## Payments

### Recording a Payment

1. Tap on a shop to open its details
2. Tap **Add Payment**
3. Enter the payment amount
4. Optionally select payment method (cash, card, bank transfer)
5. Tap **Save Payment**

The payment amount is automatically subtracted from the shop's balance.

### Viewing Payment History

All payments for a shop are listed in the shop's detail page.

---

## Understanding Balance

The balance shows how much a shop owes you:

```
Balance = Total Orders - Total Payments
```

Example:
- Shop has 3 orders totaling Rs. 5,000
- Shop has made payments totaling Rs. 3,000
- Current balance: Rs. 2,000

---

## Tips for Fast Use

- **Large buttons** - Designed for easy tapping on mobile
- **Minimal typing** - Most actions require just a few taps
- **Search first** - Use search to quickly find shops
- **Default quantities** - Product prices are pre-set for quick order entry
